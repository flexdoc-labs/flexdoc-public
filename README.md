# Flexdoc Public

Flexdoc is a specialist property finance platform for self-made Australians.

We help clients navigate property finance with clarity, structure and specialist credit assistance, with a focus on SMSF property finance, investment lending and complex borrower scenarios.

This public repository shares non-confidential information about:
- who we are
- the problems we are working on
- how we think about specialist property finance
- educational resources for borrowers and partners
- public research themes

This repository does not contain:
- client information
- lender-specific intelligence
- internal strategy
- private workflows
- proprietary credit models
- confidential deal information

## Public purpose

This repository exists to make Flexdoc more open, discoverable and useful without exposing private operating intelligence.

Our private operating systems, lender notes, deal reviews, workflow intelligence and internal strategy remain separate.
