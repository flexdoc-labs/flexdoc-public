# Who We Are

Flexdoc is a specialist property finance platform based in Sydney, Australia.

We work with self-made Australians who are building wealth through property and need clear, structured credit assistance.

Our focus includes:
- SMSF property finance
- investment property lending
- complex borrower scenarios
- self-employed and commercially minded clients
- property finance structures that require specialist handling

## Our role

Flexdoc provides credit assistance and lending support.

We help clients understand lending pathways, prepare their finance position and work through lender requirements.

We do not provide:
- financial product advice
- superannuation advice
- tax advice
- legal advice
- investment advice

Clients should seek independent professional advice from appropriately licensed advisers before making decisions about superannuation, tax, legal structures or investments.

## Why we exist

Property finance can be unnecessarily complex, especially for borrowers with ambition, irregular income, SMSF structures or investment goals.

Flexdoc exists to bring more clarity, structure and confidence to that process.
